WELCOME!! :)

This is a 1V1 2D pixel game. You will be teleported to a forest surrounded by dangerous creatures. Use your weapon to fight as many monsters as possible. Survive until the last second, good luck!

How To Play

1. New players can register for a new account; existing players can sign in with your account.

2. The game starts in 5s after both players are ready.

3. There are two characters: Swordsman and Berserker, use " ← ↑ → " to control the movement.

4. Hold your weapons tightly and use "space" to attack.

5. You only have 3♥, keep an eye on useful items or threatening traps on the ground!
